About Us

Located in the heart of the Silicon Valley

MY SWING STUDIO is a full service indoor golf studio providing personalized instruction and practice for all ages and skill levels. Using state of the art Foresight GCquad launch monitor technology featuring ball and club head data, our goal is to provide a tailored golf program to fit the specific goals of each individual. Our philosophy is that playing better golf makes the game more enjoyable. Not all individuals have the same physical abilities, so here at MY SWING STUDIO it's our job to help everyone uncover their strengths and weaknesses and together build a solid foundation for the movements required for an effective golf swing.

Key Features:

  • Foresight GCquad launch monitor technology
  • Ball and club head data
  • Tailored golf program to fit specific goals
