About Us

MY SWING STUDIO is a full service indoor golf studio providing personalized instruction and practice for all ages and skill levels. Using state of the art Foresight GCquad Rangex launch monitor technology featuring ball and club head data, our goal is to provide a tailored golf program to fit the specific goals of each individual. Our philosophy is that playing better golf makes the game more enjoyable. Not all individuals have the same physical abilities, so here at MY SWING STUDIO it's our job to help everyone uncover their strengths and weaknesses and together build a solid foundation for the movements required for an effective golf swing.

Practice

Rangex golf technology offers a cutting-edge solution for golfers aiming to enhance their game through precise data analysis. Utilizing a patented ‘eXact Raw’ algorithm and a custom-built Triple Camera system, Rangex captures detailed 3D data on both ball flight and club movement, surpassing traditional doppler radar and camera-based devices in accuracy . This includes real-time measurements of ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, and club path, providing golfers with comprehensive insights into their performance . The system also features visual data tools, such as swing motion comparisons and shot history, enabling players to identify and address specific areas for improvement . With an intuitive user interface and customizable training environments, Rangex empowers golfers to focus solely on their swing, making practice sessions more efficient and effective.

Fitness and Training

Fitness training can significantly enhance your golf game by improving strength, flexibility, balance, and endurance. Targeted exercises for the core, hips, and shoulders help generate more power and stability in your swing, leading to greater distance and control. Additionally, better overall fitness reduces the risk of injury and keeps you focused and energized throughout a full round. Incorporating a regular fitness routine tailored to golf can give you a competitive edge and make the game more enjoyable.
